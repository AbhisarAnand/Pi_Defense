Pi_Defense

Mask and Occupation Detector


A Machine/Deep learning product that is able to detect if a person is wearing a mask or not while keeping track of the number of people entering and exiting the temple.

Our Product

  • Detects people that are not wearing a mask when they enter the temple.
    • If a person without a mask is detected, a customizable message is played to alert the person to wear a mask.
  • It also keeps account for the number of people that are in the temple.
    • When the max occupancy is reached, a customizable message is played when the next person is detected to let them know that the temple is currently full and they need to wait until someone leaves.
